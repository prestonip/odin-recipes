Chili

Black Bean Chili

Very spicy and full of protein

Goes well with tortilla chips

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef/turkey
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • paprika
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • Optional Ingredients
    • cheddar cheese, shredded
    • green onion, chopped
    • tortilla chips
    • sour cream

Cooking Instructions

  1. Add ground meat to skillet and heat at medium heat. Thoroughly break up meat and cook until totally brown, then turn heat to low and drain.
  2. Add spices to taste and mix thoroughly.
  3. Drain black beans and add to skillet. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Add diced tomatoes to skillet. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Turn off heat. Serve with chips, cheese green onion, or sour cream as desired!
